About

What is WriteID?

WriteID is a state-of-the-art, AI-powered handwriting recognition and verification platform. It combines deep learning with hybrid image analysis to determine authenticity, detect forgeries, and assist in forensic document examination.

Document Verification

Verify handwriting consistency across legal agreements, court records, manuscripts, and historical documents with confidence scores.

Signature Authentication

Confirm the authenticity of signatures and sign-offs. Detect traced, forged, or replicated signatures instantly.

Forensic Analysis

Examine ink stroke patterns with analytical hybrid matching — producing detailed sub-metric scoring for legal and forensic use.

Capabilities

Core Features

Everything you need for professional handwriting analysis

AI Engine

Siamese Neural Network

MobileNetV2-based deep learning model compares unique stroke features, spacing, and curve patterns across handwriting samples.

Security

Forgery Detection

Classifies handwriting as Genuine, Forged, or Traced with a detailed confidence score and forgery probability index.

Reporting

Detailed Similarity Report

Breaks down results into stroke similarity, character spacing, writing style, and an overall confidence percentage.

Image Processing

Smart Enhancement Pipeline

Bilateral filtering, CLAHE contrast, and auto-deskewing prepare every sample before analysis for maximum accuracy.

Capture

Mobile Camera Capture

Use your device's camera to capture handwriting directly. Auto-crop detects document boundaries and extracts the writing area.

Language

Multi-Language Support

Supports English and regional scripts — designed to handle diverse writing systems without language-specific tuning.
